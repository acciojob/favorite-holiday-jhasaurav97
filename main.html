Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Often referred to as the "City of Light," Paris attracts millions of visitors each year with its rich history and romantic charm.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to world-famous artworks including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A scenic avenue perfect for shopping and strolling.
  5. Montmartre – A charming hilltop neighborhood with artistic heritage.
